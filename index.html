FAQ

Common Questions

How fast do we start?
We launch within 10–14 days after onboarding is complete. Our team moves fast — you'll have a 90-day plan, funnel structure, and campaign setup ready before most agencies even finish their intake call.
Do you require long contracts?
No. All services are month-to-month with a 30-day cancellation notice. We earn your continued business through results, not contractual obligations.
Who works on my account?
A dedicated pod assigned to your brand: a Project Manager, Client Success Coordinator, Designer, Copywriter, Automation Specialist, and Media Buyer — all working under a single unified strategy.
Do you offer white-label services?
Yes — we offer fully white-label services for agencies and consultants who want to resell our capabilities under their own brand. Contact us for partnership details.
Do you create content?
Yes — we produce up to 30–60 content pieces per month depending on your plan: short-form videos, reels, carousels, ad creatives, scripts, and more. All powered by AI and refined by our creative team.
What industries do you specialize in?
HVAC & Home Services, Real Estate, E-commerce, Coaching & Consulting, Construction & Trades, Transportation & Logistics, Medical & MedSpa, and Auto Repair. Each industry has a pre-built campaign kit ready to deploy.
What's the minimum budget to work with XSellera?
Our Starter Package begins at $1,400/mo. We recommend a minimum ad spend of $1,500–$3,000/mo on top of the management fee to see meaningful results. Book a strategy call and we'll help you find the right fit.
