2. Services Provided

XSellera provides marketing, branding, automation, advertising, creative, consulting, operational development, team sourcing, and related digital services. All services are defined in a signed Statement of Work (SOW).

3. Payment Terms

All payments are due in advance, monthly, unless otherwise stated. Payment methods accepted: ACH, Credit Card, Wire, Stripe, PayPal, Wise.

  • If payment is late by 10 days, XSellera may terminate services.

All services are month-to-month unless otherwise stated in the SOW. Either party may terminate with 30 days' notice.

Both parties agree not to disclose confidential or proprietary information to third parties.

XSellera is not liable for loss of revenue, loss of profits, claims due to ad platform decisions, delays caused by Client's inaction, third-party tool outages, algorithm changes, or force majeure. Maximum liability is limited to one month of fees paid.
